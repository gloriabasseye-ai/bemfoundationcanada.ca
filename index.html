Our Programs

Youth Mentorship

We walk alongside young people as they figure out school, friendships, and their future. Through one-on-one mentoring, life-skills workshops, and peer support circles, youth gain confidence, build positive habits, and learn real-world skills like job prep and money management. Our mentors are also trained to support youth who have faced difficult experiences, including exploitation or trafficking.

Family Support

Raising a family in Canada can be overwhelming. We offer parenting workshops, help families access affordable child care, and host family days where parents can meet trusted child care providers and community workers. We also provide safe, confidential support for families welcoming back loved ones who have survived exploitation or trafficking.

RESET Initiative

Everyone deserves a second chance. RESET supports youth who have had involvement with the justice system, offering weekly coaching, job readiness training, and positive opportunities to build resilience. We focus on giving young people the tools to move forward with dignity and purpose.

Settlement Support

Starting over in a new country is never easy. Our settlement program helps newcomers understand Canadian systems, practice their English, and connect with housing, jobs, and community resources. We also provide specialized support for newcomer survivors of trafficking, ensuring safety, belonging, and a fresh start.
